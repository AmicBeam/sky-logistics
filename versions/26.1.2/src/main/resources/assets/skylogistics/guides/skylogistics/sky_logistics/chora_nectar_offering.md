# Chora Nectar Offering

<ItemLink id="chora_nectar" /> is a core Sky Logistics material, shaped around containment, storage, and expandable space. It is not only used for expansion; it also appears in base recipes for sky containers, nodes, and upgrades. It is made through sky offering.

The Chora Nectar recipe requires a tier 2 altar. Place a charged Eulogia Crystal on the altar, then place 4 Dragon's Breath, 4 Diamonds, 4 Amethyst Shards, and 1 Glass Bottle on the four offering tables in any order.

This recipe has a duration of 240 ticks. When the ritual completes, the offerings and crystal are consumed and Chora Nectar is created on the altar. If the altar slot cannot hold it, the item drops above the altar.
